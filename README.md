## Overview

Potio ("the App") is a tattoo aftercare companion app developed by Majestica.
We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**Potio does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All information you enter in the app — including your tattoo date, skin type,
body placement, wrap type, and care session history — is stored exclusively
on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

This data never leaves your device. We have no servers, no accounts,
and no databases that store your information.

## Third-Party Services

Potio does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks,
or tracking services.

## Notifications

If you grant permission, the app sends local notifications to remind you
of care steps. These notifications are generated entirely on your device
and are never transmitted to any external server.

## Apple Watch

If you use the optional Apple Watch companion app, data is synced
between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity
framework. This data stays within your personal Apple ecosystem
and is never accessible to us.

## Children's Privacy

Potio does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will
be reflected on this page with an updated date.
